Miss Camille

Miss Camille is a universe of bohemian and vintage inspiration. The smell of patchouli is revealed when you open the treasure box in the attic. Vintage elements play with graphic charms, two worlds meet to form the creations of Miss Camille's creations. These creations are born from the desire to create modern and sparkling modern and sparkling jewelry by looking for materials that are close to my heart.

To always reinvent yourself, you must be nourished by the colors of nature nature, the sound of emotions. A song, a painting, a movie a film can inspire a new collection. It is necessary to let its to let your creativity flow freely to create jewels in your own our image.
